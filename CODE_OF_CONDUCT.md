# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

EchoPost is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for all community members, regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Experience level
- Immigration status
- Marital status
- National origin
- Personal appearance
- Political belief
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation
- Socioeconomic status

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

All community members are expected to:

- **Be respectful** - Treat others with courtesy and respect
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome people of all backgrounds
- **Be collaborative** - Work together toward common goals
- **Be constructive** - Provide helpful feedback and criticism
- **Be patient** - Understand that people have different experience levels
- **Listen actively** - Hear others' perspectives without judgment
- **Assume good intent** - Interpret actions positively when possible

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable:

- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- Discriminatory jokes or slurs
- Offensive comments related to protected characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive comments
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing private information without permission
- Threats or violent language
- Spam or excessive self-promotion
- Any other conduct that violates community standards

## Community Guidelines

### In Issues & Discussions

- Stay on topic
- Search before posting duplicates
- Provide context and details
- Use threads for replies
- Be respectful of maintainers' time

### In Pull Requests

- Follow code style guidelines
- Provide clear descriptions
- Respond to feedback respectfully
- Don't dismiss concerns
- Celebrate accepted PRs together

### In Chat (Discord, etc.)

- Keep channels on-topic
- Use appropriate channels
- Avoid spam and self-promotion
- Respect others' time zones
- Be mindful of notification spam

### On Social Media

- Represent the project respectfully
- Don't speak for maintainers
- Share constructive feedback
- Give credit to contributors
- Avoid drama and conflicts

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document** the incident (screenshots, links, times)
2. **Email** the report to: conduct@echopost.dev
3. **Include**:
   - What happened
   - When and where
   - Who was involved
   - Impact on you
   - Any witnesses
   - Preferred resolution

### Confidentiality

Reports are:
- Treated confidentially
- Handled by designated team members
- Investigated thoroughly
- Resolved in private

### Response Process

1. **Acknowledgment** - We'll confirm receipt within 48 hours
2. **Investigation** - We'll gather information and context
3. **Resolution** - We'll take appropriate action
4. **Follow-up** - We'll check in on your wellbeing

### Possible Actions

Depending on severity and history:

- **Warning** - Private discussion about behavior
- **Temporary Ban** - Excluded from community for a period
- **Permanent Ban** - Removed from community
- **Legal Action** - For serious violations

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub repositories and issues
- Pull requests and discussions
- Discord server and chat
- Social media representing the project
- In-person events
- Email communications
- Any official project spaces
